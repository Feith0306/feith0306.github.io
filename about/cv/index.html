Research Experience

Adaptive Backstepping Control for Constrained Uncertain Nonlinear Systems

  • Adaptive neural control for nonlinear constrained systems with unknown parameters, stochastic disturbances, and unknown control directions.
  • Command-filtered and compensation-based control for heterogeneous switched multi-agent systems and time-delay dynamics.
  • Stability, constraint satisfaction, synchronization, and robust H-infinity tracking under Lyapunov and zero-sum game formulations.

Projected Self-Triggered Reinforcement Learning for Differential Games

  • Self-triggered learning frameworks for zero-sum and nonzero-sum differential games.
  • Projection-operator-based Actor-Critic and Actor-Critic-Disturbance learning to prevent parameter drift.
  • Resource-aware Nash approximation with intermittent feedback, fuzzy logic, experience replay, bounded errors, and Zeno-free execution.

Data-Efficient Composite Actor-Critic Reinforcement Learning

  • Composite Actor-Critic learning for multi-player nonzero-sum differential games.
  • Finite/sufficient excitation conditions to relax persistence of excitation.
  • Critic/actor weight convergence and approximate Nash equilibrium learning for nonlinear multi-agent decision systems.
